Our Identity

Who We Are

Moi Primary & Junior School Kabarak was established in {{ settings.get('founded','1991') }} on the Kabarak University campus in Nakuru County. Over more than three decades, MPSK has grown into one of the region's most trusted primary institutions — offering CBC education from PP1 to Grade 9 within a safe, faith-centred environment.

As an affiliate of Kabarak University, we are guided by the principle of Education in Biblical Perspective — forming learners who are not only academically excellent but morally grounded, socially responsible, and purpose-driven.
