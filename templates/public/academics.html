Curriculum

Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC)

The CBC, introduced by Kenya's Ministry of Education, moves beyond rote memorisation to develop seven core competencies in every learner: Communication & Collaboration, Critical Thinking & Problem-Solving, Creativity & Imagination, Digital Literacy, Learning to Learn, Self-Efficacy, and Citizenship.

At MPSK, we deliver the CBC with experienced teachers, rich learning materials, and pastoral support — grounded in the faith values of our Kabarak University parent institution.
